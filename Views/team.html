Meet the Miri Team

Ramel Sanchez

Owner | CEO
Producer and multi-instrumentalist Ramel Sanchez is the owner and visionary behind Miri.

At a young age, Ramel started recording and mixing his own songs, which spanned an eclectic mix of rock, hip-hop, and EDM. He further pursued his passion and developed his musical skills as a band member and arranger for several notable acts, including Oxeye Daisy (drums, guitar), Nuancer (synth, bass), Ghost Revue (bass) and Babelord (production). In addition to touring throughout the US and the UK, he has several credits to his name as a guest musician.

Ramel opened his studio in 2018, with an eye towards helping performing artists like himself seeking affordable mixing and mastering. That project soon expanded to include live Miri Music video sessions, co-directed and produced by talents including Jake Cox, Andy Borgione, and Stephen Pamas. These special live performances are carefully shot in a unique setting and then masterfully edited to ensure artists look as good as they sound.

Andy Borgione

Photographer / Videographer
Andy Borgione is a Producer, Photographer, & Videographer. He has been creating photo and video content with local bands since moving to Denver in 2014. His work has been featured in Westword & 303 Magazine. In his professional life, Andy is a Producer at AOR, a local advertising agency, where he leads photo and video projects for a huge range of clients.

Jake Cox

Photographer / Videographer
Jake Cox is a photographer, retoucher and visual artist from Denver. He’s been doing photography for 10 years and is mainly self taught. He has a cat named Jeff, loves hockey and collecting vinyI. He currently works as an editorial and commerical photographer and as a retoucher in the Denver Metro area doing advertising photography. He also loves food, romantic walks on the beach and will debate you on which Beatles album is the best.

Stephen Pamas

Audio Engineer
Stephen Pamas is a multi-instrumentalist, songwriter, producer, and engineer who has been involved in the Denver music scene since 2015. His experience with live sound, lyricism, and song crafting allows him to drive your song to new heights and brings out that special sauce that listeners can’t get enough of.
