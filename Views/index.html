Let’s make something you are going to love.

Miri Music is a full-service recording studio in Colorado's Denver Metro Area which provides professional and affordable recording, mixing, mastering and production in a welcoming, creative atmosphere.

Recording

We have built a customized space with the artist in mind so you can unleash the musical sleeping giants within you.

Mixing

No matter the system, our team will engineer punchier kicks, thicker bass, dreamier guitars, and vocals that break hearts.

Mastering

Our team will transform your album or single into a full-bodied and cohesive master that is ready to distribute.

Live Videos

Our expert videographers will make you look hot as hell with live film session customized around your vibe.
